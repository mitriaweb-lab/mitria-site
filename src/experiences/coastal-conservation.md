Discover the island of Koh Klang, where community, culture, and conservation come together. Stay at a cozy guesthouse run by locals Mat and Muna. Enjoy Muna's delicious home-cooked seafood and join Mat in learning about their inspiring work to restore the island's mangroves and wildlife populations.

Take part in traditional workshops like batik making, coconut carving, or natural dyeing, and visit projects supporting native bees and coastal ecosystems. With Mat as your guide, you'll explore the island's nature, waterways, and vibrant village life while contributing to its ongoing conservation efforts.
